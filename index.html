Products

Iphone 15

The iPhone 15 is the latest and greatest smartphone from Apple. It features a beautiful 6.1-inch Super Retina XDR display, a powerful A16 Bionic chip, and an advanced dual-camera system. It is available in five vibrant colors: Pink, Yellow, Green, Blue, and Black.

Iphone 15 plus

The iPhone 15 Plus is a high-end smartphone released by Apple in September 2023. It features a 6.7-inch OLED display, an A16 Bionic chip, a 48-megapixel main camera, a 12-megapixel ultrawide camera, and a 12-megapixel telephoto camera.

Iphone 15 pro

The iPhone 15 Pro is a high-end smartphone released by Apple in September 2023.The phone has a 6.1-inch Super Retina XDR display with ProMotion technology, an A17 Pro chip with a 6-core GPU, and a 48MP Main camera with multiple focal lengths.

Iphone 15 pro max

The iPhone 15 Pro Max has a titanium frame that makes it lighter. It has a new A17 Pro processor, a USB Type-C port, and a 5x optical telephoto lens. The phone's display is 6.69 inches diagonally and has a curved design with rounded corners.

Apple Watch Ultra 2

The Apple Watch Ultra 2 is the most rugged and capable Apple Watch to date, designed for outdoor adventures and supercharged workouts. It features a lightweight titanium case, an extra-long battery life, and the brightest-ever display.

Apple AirPods Pro 2nd generation

The Apple AirPods Pro (2nd generation) are noise-canceling wireless earbuds that deliver an immersive audio experience. They feature up to 2x more Active Noise Cancellation than the previous generation, as well as Adaptive Transparency mode, which lets you hear your surroundings without removing the earbuds.
